About Me

When I first began thinking of changing careers, I knew I wanted to choose a field that is mentally demanding, constantly evolving, and never boring. After a colleague mentioned a friend who went to a coding bootcamp, I began to research the world of software engineering and found that I really enjoyed coding! Like medicine, software engineering involves critical thinking and problem solving and thanks to its ever-evolving nature, the learning never stops - as an avid and curious learner, that is what I like most about software engineering! It also allows for me to be creative in a way I was not able to be previously.

When looking for bootcamps, I knew I wanted an intense, full-time, and in-person experience. I ultimately selected App Academy for those reasons – going through the program was more challenging than I expected, which made it even more rewarding. My favorite part of the experience was pair programming with my fellow students – working together to solve problems and hearing them share their perspectives allowed me to learn faster and become a better coder.

I am excited to join the tech field as a software engineer and hope to use my knowledge of both coding and medicine to improve medical technologies and software for fellow doctors. I enjoy both front and back-end development and am looking forward to working with a team of like-minded individuals!
